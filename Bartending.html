Course at a glance: 6‑week bar mastery

From bar basics to advanced mixology, live service simulations, and operational readiness — every week builds real-world bartending competence.

Week 1
Bar basics & safety
Bar stations, tools, glassware, setup discipline. Hygiene standards, responsible service principles, and opening/closing procedures.
  • Bar equipment familiarization
  • Mise en place & workflow
  • Safety & responsible serving
Week 2
Classic cocktails & technique
Shaking, stirring, layering, measuring, garnishing. Core cocktail families (Margarita, Old Fashioned, Negroni) and menu standards.
  • Accuracy & speed drills
  • Balance & flavor profiling
Week 3
Advanced mixology
Signature drinks, menu creativity, spirits knowledge, beverage pairing. Mocktails & alcohol-free service options.
  • Infusions & house syrups
  • Modernist techniques
Week 4
Guest service at the bar
Professional communication, VIP behaviour, upselling, team coordination. Premium hospitality etiquette for high-end lounges.
  • Reading guests & personalisation
  • Conflict resolution & service recovery
Week 5
Bar operations & control
Inventory, stock awareness, basic costing, workflow during peak service. Operational discipline for bars, lounges, and events.
  • Pour cost & waste management
  • Beverage procurement basics
Week 6
Live assessment & readiness
Practical service simulations, final drink & service assessments, career preparation for hospitality roles.
  • Speed round challenge
  • Portfolio & interview prep
