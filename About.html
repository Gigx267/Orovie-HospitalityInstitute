THE OROVIE STORY

Redefining the Standards of African Hospitality

Founded on the principles of grace, precision, and cultural richness, Orovie Hospitality Institute was established to bridge the gap between traditional service and international luxury standards.

For over a decade, we have served as a sanctuary for aspiring hoteliers and experts. Our curriculum is designed to instill a lifestyle of refinement.

Today, our alumni lead prestigious establishments globally, carrying with them the "Orovie Mark"—a signature of excellence.
